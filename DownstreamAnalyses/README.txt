########################################################################################
compareXPCLR_XPEHH.R calls peaks in both the XPCLR and XPEHH data
It requires six arguments:
 the first four are the peak calling thresholds
 the fifth is whether to smooth
 the final one is the species

########################################################################################
comparePeakCallThresh.R compares the XPCLR and XPEHH peak calls within species to see if a significant overlap
It requires seven arguments:
 the first four are the peak calling thresholds
 the next two are whether to smooth
 the final one is the species

########################################################################################
compareWBtoCow.R compares water buffalo peaks to those in cow
It takes four arguments, the peak calling thresholds
